# IMPERIUM EXECUTION LAW V1

1. No TASK_ID, no execution.
2. No STAGE_ID, no execution.
3. No RUN_ID, no execution.
4. No assigned contour, no execution.
5. No gate READY, no stage start.
6. No receipt, no completion.
7. No provenance, no artifact acceptance.
8. No ledger event, no movement.
9. No barrier, no final acceptance.
10. No fallback.
11. No latest logic.
12. No unknown addresses.
13. No silent repair.
14. No destructive action without explicit safe guard.
15. Recoverable repair must be declared, executed, verified, and logged.
16. Fatal conflict stops the task.
17. Owner decision is required when policy cannot safely decide.
18. Servitor must wait when gate says WAITING.
19. Servitor must stop on BLOCKED, FAIL, CONFLICT, or TIMEOUT.
20. File existence alone is never proof of completion.
