# KIRO OUTPUT TABLE REQUIREMENTS V0.1

Kiro planning/review answers must be table-first. Narrative is secondary.

## Mandatory tables

### 1) Reality Table

| claim | evidence | verified/unverified | risk | implication |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### 2) Recommendation Table

| recommendation | why | target path/zone | checker | receipt | Owner gate | risk if skipped |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### 3) Task Decomposition Table

| task candidate | purpose | stages | allowed files | forbidden files | acceptance criteria | blockers |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### 4) Red-Team Table

| failure mode | how it appears | detection gate | mitigation | severity |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### 5) Do Now / Delay / Reject Table

| item | decision | reason | dependency | Owner approval needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### 6) Questions Table

| question | why it matters | what breaks without answer | who decides |
|---|---|---|---|
