## Final Architecture Summary

**Doctrinarium should be:**

> **The law enforcement and organ health gate of IMPERIUM** — a working organ that stores machine-readable laws with explicit enforcement modes, evaluates organ health through file-based self-reports, guards the doctrine base against corruption and contradiction, and issues task-start verdicts that either allow execution, warn with evidence, or block with explicit blockers. It prepares Inquisition escalation packets but keeps the hook disabled until Inquisition is built. It does not own task decomposition (Astronomicon), memory (Administratum), roles (Officio Agentis), or audit verdicts (Inquisition). It is severe about fake green: no PASS without evidence, no HEALTHY without checker run, no disabled hook pretending to be active.
