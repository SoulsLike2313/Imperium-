
# IMPERIUM OPERATIONALITY 70 WHOLE-REPO FUSION ROADMAP V0.1

## Strategic Goal
- Reach approximately 70% operational executability quickly, with truth-bound execution, observability, control, and delivery discipline.
- Keep Owner in command through a visible, configurable control center that can accept tasks, choose path, execute, warn, prove, package, and deliver.

## Current Starting Point
- Gate Spine baseline exists (`U00-U13`, including report output budget and Python type-safety promotion law).
- Script pain has been captured into doctrine/policy/backlog.
- Second Brain runtime truth is partially established but still blocked for full UI performance acceptance due static asset route truth gaps.
- Control Center, Agent Factory, Freelance Corridor, and Deliverable Factory are not yet implemented as operational systems.

## Why Work Now Spans The Whole Repository
- Owner strategy shifted from isolated `IMPERIUM_TEST_VERSION/SECOND_BRAIN` work to whole-body fusion: canon doctrine, organs, gates, tools, test technology absorption, and future delivery corridors.
- Local optimization without whole-repo fusion would create parallel realities, duplicate logic, and fake progress.

## Fusion Principle: Canon + Test Technology
- Main/canon law defines truth gates, ownership, and acceptance rules.
- Test-version technologies provide proven experiments and tooling candidates.
- Fusion rule: absorb through Mechanicus/Scriptorium classification, gating, and receipts; never blind copy.

## Stage Status Semantics
- `PASS`: stage objectives and required evidence are complete and auditable.
- `WARN`: stage advanced but has bounded risks or incomplete side conditions.
- `BLOCKED`: hard blocker prevents safe continuation of stage objective.
- `UNKNOWN`: insufficient evidence yet.

## Why These Stages Are Ordered This Way
- Truth and doctrine first, then gates, then absorption, then runtime truth closure, then data spine, then control surfaces, then production corridors, then fusion cleanup.
- This sequencing maximizes leverage while minimizing fake progress and rework.

## Adaptive Correction Loop
- Any repeated pain, blocker, or ambiguity must be converted into one of:
  - doctrine update;
  - new/expanded gate;
  - backlog item;
  - checker/report improvement.
- Owner corrections are not commentary only; they become executable law artifacts.

## Preservation And Expansion Of Original Gate Spine + Second Brain Plan
- Original Gate Spine + Second Brain PDF remains valid as foundational corridor.
- This roadmap preserves that spine and expands it to whole-repo governance, control center doctrine, delivery factories, and agent factory compliance.
