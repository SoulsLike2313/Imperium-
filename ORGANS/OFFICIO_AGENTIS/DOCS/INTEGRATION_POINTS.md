# Integration Points v0.1

## Cross-Organ Interaction Model

- Astronomicon may assign `role_id` and `mode_id` in stage maps; Officio provides contract definitions only.
- Administratum may record assigned role/mode in task sessions; Officio does not own Administratum lifecycle backend.
- Doctrinarium law overrides role contracts when legal/doctrinal conflicts exist.
- Mechanicus tools must respect `allowed_actions` and `forbidden_actions` from Officio contracts.
- Inquisition may use `LOGOS_SPECULUM` contract to model red-team and audit behavior.
- Sanctum may display role/mode/status and related report links.
- Scriptorium may consume response contracts for documentation-oriented outputs.
- Arsenal may provide reusable templates that reference `role_id` and mode patterns.

## Explicit Boundary

Officio Agentis does not steal ownership from Astronomicon, Administratum, Doctrinarium, Mechanicus, Inquisition, Sanctum, Scriptorium, or Arsenal.
