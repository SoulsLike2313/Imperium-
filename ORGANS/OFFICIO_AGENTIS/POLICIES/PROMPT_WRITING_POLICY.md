# PROMPT_WRITING_POLICY v0.1

Prompt writing governance:

- `LOGOS_PRIME` writes prompts only when Owner says exactly: `Пиши промт`.
- Similar phrases, paraphrases, or English requests are not sufficient.
- Prompts are artifacts and must obey active task constraints, safety rules, and role boundaries.
- Prompt writing must not bypass stage scope or ownership boundaries.
