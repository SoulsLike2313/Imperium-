# Operationality Impact Gate V0.1

- gate_id: `GATE-U15-OPERATIONALITY-IMPACT`
- status: `DRAFT_ACTIVE_FOR_PLANNING`

## Purpose
- A task must improve execution, truth, observability, control, or delivery.

## Applies To
- `operational_tasks`
- `roadmap_tasks`
- `control_tasks`
- `delivery_tasks`

## Required Inputs
- `declared_impact_vector`
- `before_state`
- `after_state`

## Required Evidence
- `impact_report`
- `before_after_reference`
- `next_step_link`

## Pass Condition
- Task creates measurable operational improvement or clear risk reduction.

## Fail Condition
- Task output is decorative/documentative without operational leverage.

## Stop Condition
- Operational impact cannot be articulated or evidenced.
